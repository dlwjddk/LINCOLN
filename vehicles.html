NAVIGATOR

LINCOLN EMBRACE

You’re near and your Navigator can tell. When it senses your approach,
a suite of features activates as if to say “Welcome back” and “Where to next?”

AUTOMATIC POWER-FOLDING MIRRORS

With automatic power-folding sideview mirrors engaged, your Navigator can help prevent accidental nicks and
dings. When you enter the vehicle — with your key fob or using your Phone As A Key —
and close the doors, the sideview mirrors automatically fold out.
As you exit and lock the doors, the mirrors return to their folded-in position.
