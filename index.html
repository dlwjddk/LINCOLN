VEHICLES

With standard Lincoln Connect,™* you and your passengers can enjoy seamless connectivity. All models feature a 4G LTE Wi-Fi hotspot that allows up to 10 devices to be connected at the same time, and comes with a 3-month/ 3-gigabyte trial. Plus, with the embedded modem, you can use the Lincoln Way™ App to its fullest and even keep track of your Wi-Fi data usage. Whether in the vehicle or up to 50 feet away, you can access the hotspot and make connections.
