ABOUT CERTIFIED PRE-OWNED

The benefits that come from owning a Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned vehicle will exceed your expectations.
The privilege of Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned ownership is our collective commitment to be there for you and your Lincoln.

Lincoln worked closely with our dealerships to develop and implement enhanced vehicle cleaning measures, guided by CDC recommendations, as part of our Lincoln Pickup & Delivery experience.* In addition, we are providing our dealers with personal protection equipment (PPE) for their staff and guests. If you have any questions about the process or disinfectants used, feel free to reach out to your dealership representative.

  • CARFAX® VEHICLE HISTORY REPORT™

    Not every vehicle can make the cut as a carefully selected Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned vehicle. In fact, to even be considered for certification, each Lincoln vehicle must be less than six model years old and have fewer than 60,000 miles on the odometer.

  • 24-HOUR ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE

    Unlike used vehicles, Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned vehicles come with complimentary roadside assistance – please call the Lincoln Customer Relationship Center at (800) 521-4140. Fuel delivery if the vehicle runs out of fuel — up to two gallons at no charge.

  • DESTINATION EXPENSE ASSISTANCE

    Up to $500 – for up to three days’ lodging, meals and rental vehicle for travel expenses accrued if the breakdown occurs more than 100 miles from home. Towing assistance of up to $100 for warranty and non-warranty problems, including accidents
